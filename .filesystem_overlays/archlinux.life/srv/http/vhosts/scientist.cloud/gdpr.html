We're glad you're here, that means you care about your data!

First of all and most importantly, we'll never give your information to any third parties unless a crime has been committed!
We will also do our best to keep your information and integrity safe and promise to never sell your data to third parties.


To keep this short, we save your data in order to process orders and messages between you and us.
We only save your data (more on what type of data we save later) in these cases:
  • When processing an order
  • While a conversation is active using our contact forms
  • To analyze, track or process IT-related tasks such as incidents or debugging.

While processing an order

When your order is complete, and you've acknowledged delivery, you'll be asked to save your data with us.
The default action after delivery of a bike is that we remove data related to you as an individual within 90 days to comply with GDPR.
This means we'll never be able to go back to your order and see information regarding you as a customer in the order.
We will still keep the unique order number, and what the order contained (long range, etc) for statistics.
However, if you opt in to save your data with us - we'll save it until you request us to delete it.
This would help us in contacting you in case of security patches for the ECU or in case of faulty hardware from the supplier etc.
You can also opt in to get regular service reminders on your bike for instance. Below we'll outline more on what data we save.

(To be more technical, we also delete your data from backups so there is no way for us to restore your information once gone)

While communicating with us

We will keep some information about you such as contact information in order to communicate with you.
After for instance a question has been answered or an issue with our product have been resolved,
you will recieve a log of our conversation via e-mail after which we will delete the information surrounding that dialogue.
If you have an active order, we'll still keep information regarding the order in accordance with the above section.

IT-related logs and data

When you use our services, traces that could identify you will be logged in our systems.
Such data can be your IP, your browsing behavior on our site and possible data you submit using any of our forms.
We retain this data for up to 5 years in order to comply with local logging rules. These logs might be handed out to authorities if a crime has been committed.

What data do we keep?

We save but not limited to the following data:
  • Personal number
  • E-mail address
  • Phone number
  • Order numbers
  • BankID transaction ID's (Internally called orderRef)
  • Shipping addresses
  • Payment and transactions history
  • Any data you supply freely in forms or otherwise
  • Your IP Address used when communicating with our servers
  • Any type of HTTP-related data used to communicate with our servers
And any type of data related to these (status changes, time stamps etc).

Exceptions to the ahove

Any financial records between you and us (Ond Vetenskapsman) will be saved for up to 15 years regardless of you requesting to delete your data.
The information in these financial records will contain contact information such as name and address.
This in order to comply with local financial laws.
